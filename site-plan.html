Overview

Purpose

The purpose of Typology Nexus is to provide a one-stop shop for everything typology, provided they are my own explanations but will have library integration. It acts sort of as a specialized wiki, explaining and breaking down aspects of Socionics, Enneagram, and Jung, as well as fictional archetypes and possibly even Psychosophy.

Audience

Audience here would be anyone on PDB, Reddit, or anyone interested in typology as a whole.
